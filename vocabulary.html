Vocabulary

Build your English vocabulary for real life situations. Each word includes a simple meaning and a natural example sentence.

A. Family & People

  • Parents: mother and father — My parents support me in everything I do.
  • ____________________________
  • Classmate: a student in the same class — My classmate helps me with homework.
  • Neighbor: a person living near your house — Our neighbors are very friendly.
  • Police Officer: whose job is to make people obey the law and to prevent and solve crime; the people who work for this organization.
  • Teacher: a person whose job is teaching, especially in a school.
  • Libraian: a person who is in charge of or works in a library.

B. Places in the Community

  • Hospital: a place for medical care — My brother went to the hospital for a check-up.
  • Restaurant: a place to eat — We had dinner at a Thai restaurant.
  • Bus Station: where you catch a bus — I wait at the bus station every morning.

C. Food & Drinks

  • Vegetables: healthy plants we eat — Eating vegetables is good for your body.
  • Juice: fruit drink — She drinks orange juice every morning.
  • Noodles: common Asian dish — I ordered spicy noodles for lunch.

D. Daily Activities

  • Exercise: move your body to be healthy — I exercise three times a week.
  • Review: study again to remember — We review our lessons before the test.
  • Prepare: get ready — He prepares his school bag at night.
