Grammar

Learn useful grammar with clear rules and examples.

A. Simple Present Tense

Use: habits, facts, routines.

Examples: I study English every evening. She works at the library.

B. Simple Past Tense

Use: actions that happened before.

Examples: I watched a movie yesterday. They traveled to Chiang Mai last month.

C. Present Continuous Tense

Use: actions happening now.

Examples: I am reading a book. They are preparing for a presentation.

D. Future Tense (Will / Going to)

Will: decision made now. Going to: plan.

Examples: I will help you with your project. We are going to visit Bangkok next week.

E. Modal Verbs (Can / Must / Should)

Can: ability — She can speak English well.

Must: strong need — Students must wear school uniforms.

Should: advice — You should drink more water.
