Necessary tenses in daily life

Pick an essential tense with a quick structure and examples.

Present Simple

Structure: Subject + V1 (add s/es with he/she/it). Use: facts/routines.

I teach every morning. She works in Kamphaeng.

Past Simple

Structure: Subject + V2. Use: finished past actions.

We visited Satun last week.

Future Simple

Structure: Subject + will + V1. Use: plans/decisions at the moment.

I will send the report tonight.

Present Perfect

Structure: Subject + have/has + V3. Use: results/experiences connected to now.

She has finished the worksheet.
