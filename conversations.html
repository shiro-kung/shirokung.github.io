Daily Conversations

Practice useful conversations for real-life situations.

A. Greetings & Small Talk

A: Hi! How’s everything?

B: I’m doing well. How about you?

A: I’m good, thanks.

B. Asking for Directions

A: Excuse me, how can I get to the library?

B: Walk straight and turn left.

C. At a Restaurant

A: What do you recommend?

B: The fried rice is very popular.

D. School Situations

A: Did you finish the homework?

B: Not yet. I’m still working on it.

E. Making Plans

A: Are you free this weekend?

B: Yes, why?

F. Shopping

A: How much is this shirt?

B: It’s 250 baht.

G. At the Doctor

A: What seems to be the problem?

B: I have a headache.

H. At the Airport

A: Where is the check‑in counter?

B: It’s on the second floor.

I. Buying Food & Drinks

A: One iced tea, please.

B: Sweet or less sweet?

J. Talking About Hobbies

A: What do you like to do?

B: I enjoy playing guitar.

K. Asking for Help

A: Can you help me with this?

B: Sure, what do you need?

L. Talking About Weather

A: It’s really hot today.

B: Yes, maybe it will rain.

M. Making a Phone Call

A: Hello, is this Anna?

B: Yes, speaking.

N. At the Library

A: Where can I find English books?

B: They’re on the second shelf.

O. Weekend Plans

A: What are you doing this weekend?

B: I’m going to the beach.
