Daily Conversations

Practice useful conversations for real-life situations.

A. Greetings & Small Talk

A: Hi! How’s everything?

B: I’m doing well. How about you?

A: I’m good, thanks. Have you been busy lately?

B: Yes, I’ve had a lot of schoolwork. What about you?

A: Same here. I’ve been studying for exams.

B: Oh really? When is your exam?

A: Next week. I’m a bit nervous.

B: Don’t worry. I’m sure you’ll do great!

B. Asking for Directions

A: Excuse me, how can I get to the library?

B: Walk straight and turn left at the second building.

A: Okay, and is it far from here?

B: Not really. It’s about a three‑minute walk.

A: Great. Is it next to the science building?

B: Yes, exactly. You’ll see a big blue sign.

A: Thank you so much!

B: You’re welcome. Have a nice day!

C. At a Restaurant

A: What do you recommend?

B: The fried rice is very popular.

A: That sounds good. Is it spicy?

B: A little bit, but we can make it less spicy if you want.

A: Yes, please. Less spicy would be great.

B: Sure. Would you like anything to drink?

A: Just water, please.

B: Alright. Your food will be ready soon.

D. School Situations

A: Did you finish the homework?

B: Not yet. I’m still working on it.

It’s pretty difficult, right?

B: Yes, especially question number five.

A: I know! I spent almost an hour on it.

B: Really? Can you explain it to me later?

A: Sure. Let’s do it together after class.

B: Thanks! That would help a lot.

E. Making Plans

A: Are you free this weekend?

B: Yes, why?

A: I was thinking we could study English together.

B: Sounds good! Where should we meet?

A: How about the library?

B: Sure. What time?

A: Maybe 10 AM?

B: Perfect. See you then!

F. Shopping

A: How much is this shirt?

B: It’s 250 baht.

A: Do you have it in a larger size?

B: Yes, let me check… Here you go.

A: Thanks! Can I try it on?

B: Of course. The fitting room is over there.

A: Great, I’ll try it now.

G. At the Doctor

A: What seems to be the problem?

B: I have a headache and a sore throat.

A: How long have you felt this way?

B: Since yesterday afternoon.

A: Do you have a fever?

B: Yes, a little.

A: I’ll give you some medicine. Please rest and drink water.

B: Thank you, doctor.

H. At the Airport

A: Where is the check‑in counter?

B: It’s on the second floor.

A: Thank you. And where is gate 12?

B: After you check in, go straight and follow the signs.

A: Great. Is the flight on time?

B: Yes, everything is on schedule.

A: Perfect. Thanks for your help!

I. Buying Food & Drinks

A: One iced tea, please.

B: Sweet or less sweet?

A: Less sweet, please.

B: Would you like ice?

A: Yes, a little ice.

B: Anything else?

A: No, that’s all. Thank you!

J. Talking About Hobbies

A: What do you like to do?

B: I enjoy playing guitar.

A: That’s cool! How long have you played?

B: About three years.

A: Do you play every day?

B: Not every day, but at least three times a week.

A: Nice! I want to learn someday.

K. Asking for Help

A: Can you help me with this?

B: Sure, what do you need?

A: I don’t understand this question.

B: Let me see… Oh, you need to add these numbers first.

A: Ah, I get it now. Thanks!

B: No problem. Happy to help.

L. Talking About Weather

A: It’s really hot today.

B: Yes, maybe it will rain later.

A: I hope so. We need some cool air.

B: True. Yesterday was even hotter.

A: Really? I stayed inside all day.

B: Lucky you! I had to walk home.

M. Making a Phone Call

A: Hello, is this Anna?

B: Yes, speaking.

A: Are you free right now?

B: Yes, what’s up?

A: Can you send me the homework file?

B: Sure, I’ll send it to you in a minute.

A: Thank you so much!

N. At the Library

A: Where can I find English books?

B: They’re on the second shelf.

A: Thanks. Do you have any recommendations?

B: Yes, this one is great for beginners.

A: Perfect. I’ll borrow it.

B: Just bring it to the counter when you’re ready.

O. Weekend Plans

A: What are you doing this weekend?

B: I’m going to the beach.

A: That sounds fun! Who are you going with?

B: My family. We go every year.

A: Nice! I might stay home and relax.

B: That’s good too. Enjoy your weekend!
