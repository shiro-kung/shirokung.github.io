1️⃣ Read a paragraph and find the main idea

Passage:
My favorite subject is English. I like English because I can talk to people from different countries. In English class, we read stories, watch videos, and practice speaking. Learning English helps me feel more confident when I travel or talk online.

Question: What is the main idea of the paragraph?

Choices:

  • A. English class has many exams
  • B. English helps the student communicate and feel confident ✅
  • C. The student likes traveling only

2️⃣ Identify details in a description

Passage:
Anna is a college student. She studies online every morning. She uses her laptop and headphones during class. After studying, she likes to drink coffee and rest.

Questions:

  • Who is the student?
  • What does she use for class?
  • When does she study?

3️⃣ Identify the speakers in a dialogue

Dialogue:
A: Hi, are you ready for class?
B: Yes, I am. I already finished my homework.
A: Great! Let’s join the online meeting now.

Question: Who are the speakers?

Choices:

  • A. Teacher and principal
  • B. Two students talking before class ✅
  • C. Doctor and patient

4️⃣ Find 3 new vocabulary words

Passage:
Yesterday, Mark felt nervous before his presentation. He practiced many times to prepare. When it was his turn, he spoke clearly and felt proud after finishing.

Instructions:

  • Find 3 new words from the passage.
  • Write the word, meaning, and use it in your own sentence.

Example:
Nervous – feeling afraid or worried
Sentence: I feel nervous before exams.

5️⃣ Find where the story takes place (Setting)

Passage:
The students are sitting in front of their computers at home. They are listening to their teacher through a video call. Everyone is attending class online because they cannot go to school.

Question: Where does the story take place?

Correct Answer: At home / online class ✅
